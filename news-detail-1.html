1st Steering Committee - July 19th 2023

The HAPE project organized a steering committee (SC) on the 19th of July 2023. This technical committee presented to the main beneficiaries of the project, MIA, Kosovo Police (KP), Special Prosecution of the Republic of Kosovo (SPRK), Police Inspectorate of Kosovo (PIK), Kosovo Academy of Public Safety (KAPS) and Kosovo Forensic Agency (KFA), the action plan for the next 3 years, in strategic and operational support. According to multidisciplinary and cross-functional approach in many actions of the project, the support will also involve the Ministry of Justice (MJ), the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU), Kosovo Customs, the Tax Administration of Kosovo, and other relevant institutions. Regular SC will be held every four months to verify the achievements of the outputs and mandatory results and discuss future actions to be undertaken in the next following four months.
