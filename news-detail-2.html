Workshop on national strategy on drugs, from 21st to 23rd November 2023 in PRIZREN

A workshop on drafting the National Strategy against Drugs took place from the 21st to the 23rd of November 2023 and was supported with technical and administrative expertise from HAPE Project. The event commenced with the arrival and accommodation of 43 participants from various Kosovar institutions. The workshop was led by the secretariat of Strategies of the MIA during discussions and deliberations. The workshop primarily focused on the strategic aspects of combating narcotics. The second day was dedicated to the action plan and indicators and the last day was focused on the implementation plan and the monitoring of the objectives for the reporting of the next five years (six months, yearly and mid-term review)
